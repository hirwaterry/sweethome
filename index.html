Empowering Lives,
One Step at a Time

At Sweet Home Care, we are dedicated to empowering adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities, helping them achieve their fullest potential. Our approach is grounded in the principles of normalization, community integration, and self-empowerment.

By fostering engagement through community activities, we ensure that individuals are active participants in society. All of our residential and day programs are designed with these values at the core, creating supportive environments where everyone can thrive and lead fulfilling lives.

Homecare provides support programs for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities to help them live independent and meaningful lives.

We empower individuals by developing self-care, social interaction, community integration, and employment skills through specialized programs.

Our programs include behavior management, community integration, recreational activities, and skill development in advocacy and employment.

You can support us by volunteering, participating in community events, or donating through our website to help us continue our mission.
