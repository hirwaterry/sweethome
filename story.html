Our Story

At Sweet Home Care, we are committed to supporting adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities in realizing their fullest potential. Rooted in the principles of normalization, community integration, and self-empowerment, our approach focuses on creating meaningful connections and opportunities for growth.

Through active participation in community activities, we ensure that every individual is not only included but also thrives as a valued member of society. Our residential and day programs are designed around these core values, providing nurturing environments that inspire independence, confidence, and fulfillment.

At Sweet Home Care, we believe in the power of community, the dignity of every person, and the importance of creating spaces where everyone can live a meaningful and empowered life.
