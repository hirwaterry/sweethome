About Us

At Sweet Home Care, we are dedicated to empowering adults with intellectual and developmental disabilities, helping them achieve their fullest potential.

Our approach is grounded in the principles of normalization, community integration, and self-empowerment. By fostering engagement through community activities, we ensure that individuals are active participants in society.

All of our residential and day programs are designed with these values at the core, creating supportive environments where everyone can thrive and lead fulfilling lives.

Our Core Values

Empowerment

We empower individuals to reach their highest potential by fostering independence and confidence.

Community Integration

We promote meaningful participation in community life, ensuring everyone is valued and included.

Normalization

Our programs ensure individuals live in environments that reflect everyday life in the community.

Homecare

We provide comprehensive care programs that promote independence and well-being for all individuals. Join us in making a difference!
